COMPANY HISTORY

Since 2000 Narayan Engineers have established trustworthy relationships with our customers and very good rapport with almost all industrial establishments and mining industries.

A Young & Energetic Engineer having graduated from Bapuji Institute of Engineering & Technology, Davanagere, University of Mysore in the year 1987-88 in Industrial Production Engineering with the expertise in the field of Heavy Structure Fabrication for 12 years established the company M/s Narayan Engineers in 2000-2001 .
